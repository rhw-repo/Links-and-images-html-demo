About Us

Pug Talk was created by Bob the Pug in 1992. Our website still holds true to that 90s aesthetic, caputred in our glowing lack of just about everything and a ton of fugly white space and words.

Bob saw the need for pugs to be able to talk, without being harrassed by other way huger dogs in a park or some human insisting they leave the park or stop sniffing something nasty.
